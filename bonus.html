Star Fish

Starfish or sea stars are star-shaped echinoderms belonging to the class Asteroidea. Common usage frequently finds these names being also applied to ophiuroids, which are correctly referred to as brittle stars or basket stars. Starfish are also known as asteroids.

Fun Facts

  1. Starfish is not a fish
  2. They have no brain or blood.
  3. They cannot survive in fresh water.
