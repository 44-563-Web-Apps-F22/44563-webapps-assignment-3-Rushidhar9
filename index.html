Government Museum Chennai

The Government Museum, Chennai, or the Madras Museum, is a museum of human history and culture. It is particularly rich in archaeological and numismatic collection.It has the largest collection of Roman antiquities outside Europe. Among them, the colossal Museum Theatre is one of the most impressive.
